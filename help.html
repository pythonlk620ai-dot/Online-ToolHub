❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tools free to use? ▼
Yes! All our tools are completely free to use with no hidden charges or subscription fees. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.
Do I need to register or create an account? ▼
No, you don't need to register or create an account. All our tools work instantly without any personal information required.
Are my data and privacy protected? ▼
Absolutely! We don't store any of your data on our servers. All calculations are done directly in your browser, ensuring your privacy and security.
Do these tools work on mobile devices? ▼
Yes, all our tools are fully responsive and work perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
Can I download the results? ▼
For tools like QR Code Generator, yes! You can download your results. For other tools, you can easily copy the results to your clipboard using the copy button.
What browsers are supported? ▼
Our tools work on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience.
